Privacy

Last updated July 6, 2026

This is the privacy policy for leathewitch.com. The short version: this site collects very little, sells nothing about you, and would honestly rather talk about candles. The longer version follows, because the law prefers the longer version.

Who runs this site

leathewitch.com is a personal website operated by Lea ("I", "me"). For anything privacy-related, contact hello@thedarkandwild.com.

What this site collects

The site itself is a set of static pages. It has no accounts, no comment forms, and no newsletter capture. Data is collected in three ways:

  • Analytics. This site may use Google Analytics to understand which pages are read and roughly where visitors come from. Analytics data is aggregated and does not identify you personally. Google's own privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Advertising. Some pages reserve space for Google AdSense advertisements. If ads are served, Google and its partners may use cookies to show ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising at adssettings.google.com.
  • Your cookie choice. When you accept or decline the cookie notice, that choice is stored in your browser's local storage so you are not asked on every visit. It never leaves your device.

Cookies

Cookies set by the analytics and advertising services above are third-party cookies, governed by those services. You can clear or block cookies in your browser settings at any time; the site will keep working, only the cookie notice may reappear.

Shopping

Products linked from the shop are sold through The Dark and Wild on Shopify. Orders, payments, and shipping details are handled entirely there, under Shopify's checkout and that shop's own policies. This site never sees your payment information.

What this site does not do

  • It does not sell, rent, or trade personal information.
  • It does not run trackers beyond the analytics and advertising described above.
  • It does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Your rights

Depending on where you live (including the EU/EEA under the GDPR and California under the CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of personal data held about you. Since this site holds essentially none, most requests are best directed to the third-party services above — but if you write to hello@thedarkandwild.com, I will help however I can.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the new version will be posted here with an updated date at the top of the page. Continued use of the site after a change means you accept the revised policy.

Questions, corrections, or concerns: hello@thedarkandwild.com. Thank you for reading the whole thing. Almost nobody does.
